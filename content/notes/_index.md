## What These Are

**These are reference notes - not essays.** Each one explains a single economic concept: what it is, why it exists, and how it shows up in the world around you.

They are intentionally value-neutral. They do not argue for or against any policy. They simply describe how something works, so that when you encounter the concept in a full article or in the news, you already know the mechanism.
