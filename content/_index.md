## Economics for People Who Notice

Most of what you hear about the economy is wrong. Not by accident — it's wrong because someone benefits from you believing it.

**This site exists to fix that.**

### What You'll Find Here

- **[The Learning Path →](/economics/learning-path/)** — Start at the beginning if you're new. Thirteen articles, three levels, one path from confusion to clarity.
- **[All Articles →](/economics/)** — Browse the full catalog of guides and essays.
- **[About →](/about/)** — Who writes this and why.

### Why This Site?

The person who understands economics has a superpower they never knew was missing. They see through the nonsense that passes for political debate. They know who actually pays for a tax cut. They understand why rent control makes housing more expensive. They spot the bait-and-switch when someone says "the government should just..." and they know who "the government" really is.

**You don't need a degree. You just need the right handful of ideas.**

That's what this site gives you.
