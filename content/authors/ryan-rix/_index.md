**Ryan Rix** is the publisher of this site. He asks uncomfortable questions about who really benefits from government spending, corporate regulation, and the stories we tell ourselves about how the economy works.

Before running this site, Ryan spent years watching people with good intentions support policies that made things worse — and watching the people who pointed this out get called names instead of engaged. This site is his response.

He believes that most economic arguments are not really about economics. They are about who gets to decide. He thinks you should be the one who decides.
